How to play

  • Use arrow keys or WASD to move the snake
  • Near the top the screen you'll see a blue bar indicating how much energy you have. Press space to use that energy to attack opponents!
  • Collect the red dots to build up energy!
  • Watch out for the red bar below the energy bar! That displays how much damage you've taken! When it fills up your snake will grow and begin to move faster.
  • By clicking on the button under the word enemies on the right you can choose how the enemies are targeted, either randomly, by highest power, highest damage, or by manually clicking on the enemy in the right column.
